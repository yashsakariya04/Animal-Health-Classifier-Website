About AniHealth AI 📊

🤖 Advanced AI Technology

Our system uses state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms trained on extensive veterinary data to provide accurate health assessments for various animal species.

🏥 Comprehensive Analysis

We analyze multiple health parameters including blood-brain symptoms, appearance changes, general disease indicators, lung conditions, and abdominal issues.

⚡ Instant Results

Get immediate health status assessments with detailed explanations and recommendations for each animal's specific condition.

🐾 Multi-Species Support

Our AI system supports 17 different animal types including domestic pets, farm animals, and wildlife species.

📈 Data-Driven Insights

Built on comprehensive datasets with thousands of animal health records to ensure reliable and accurate predictions.

🛡️ Veterinary Grade

Developed with veterinary expertise to provide professional-grade health assessments for animal care professionals and pet owners.

How It Works ⚙️

  1. Select Animal Type: Choose from our comprehensive list of 17 different animal species including domestic pets, farm animals, and wildlife.
  2. Rate Symptom Severity: Use our intuitive 0-10 scale to rate the severity of symptoms in five key health categories: Blood-Brain, Appearance, General Disease, Lung, and Abdominal symptoms.
  3. AI Analysis: Our advanced machine learning model processes your inputs using sophisticated algorithms trained on extensive veterinary datasets.
  4. Get Detailed Results: Receive comprehensive health status assessments with specific recommendations and explanations for each animal's condition.
  5. Professional Guidance: The system provides actionable insights that can help guide veterinary care decisions and treatment planning.
